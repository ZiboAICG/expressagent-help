Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 April 2026 · Last updated: 17 April 2026

AI Consulting Group ("we", "us", "our") provides the Integrated Shopping Chatbot app (the "App") for Shopify merchants. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices merchants have when using the App.

1. Who we are

Contact us about this Privacy Policy at: info@aiconsultinggroup.com.au

2. Information we collect

  • Shop information such as store domain and app installation details
  • Product and store content needed for assistant sync
  • Merchant-entered settings, brand info, and custom rules
  • Usage logs, sync status, and error records needed to operate the app
  • Customer chat content processed on behalf of the merchant, if enabled

3. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Generate AI-assisted answers and product recommendations
  • Sync store data and save merchant configuration
  • Monitor reliability, troubleshoot issues, and maintain security
  • Respond to support requests and service-related communications

4. How we share information

We do not sell merchant data. We may share data with service providers used to operate the App, such as:

  • Hosting provider: Microsoft Azure
  • AI provider: OpenAI
  • Monitoring / analytics provider: None currently used

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the security, rights, or operation of the App.

5. Data retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the App and meet legal, security, and operational requirements. When a merchant uninstalls the App or requests deletion, we will delete or anonymize applicable data within a reasonable period.

6. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect data.

7. Merchant rights

Merchants may request access, correction, or deletion of their information by contacting info@aiconsultinggroup.com.au.

8. Policy changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Review this page once more before publishing to confirm the provider list and retention wording match your live app setup.
